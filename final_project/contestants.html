Selection Process

The celebrity selection process for Dancing With The Stars involves producers inviting well-known personalities from diverse backgrounds, such as entertainment, sports, and social media. Candidates are chosen based on their public appeal, availability, and willingness to participate. Once selected, celebrities are paired with professional dancers and undergo training before the competition begins.

Contestants

Dancing With The Stars has featured a wide range of celebrity contestants from various fields, including actors, musicians, athletes, and reality TV stars. Each season brings a new lineup of celebrities who are paired with professional dancers to compete in various dance styles. Here are some notable contestants who have participated in the show:

  • Kelly Monaco: Kelly Monaco is an actress and model who won the first season of Dancing With The Stars. She is known for her role on the soap opera "General Hospital."
  • Emmitt Smith: Emmitt Smith is a former NFL player and Pro Football Hall of Famer. He won the third season of the show and is known for his athleticism and competitive spirit.
  • Apolo Anton Ohno: Apolo Anton Ohno is an Olympic gold medalist in speed skating. He won the fourth season of Dancing With The Stars and is known for his charisma and dance skills.
  • Kendra Wilkinson: Kendra Wilkinson is a television personality and model who gained fame from the reality show "The Girls Next Door." She competed in season 12 of Dancing With The Stars.
  • Rumer Willis: Rumer Willis is an actress and the daughter of Bruce Willis and Demi Moore. She won the 20th season of the show and is known for her versatility in dance styles.
  • Jordan Fisher: Jordan Fisher is an actor, singer, and dancer who won the 25th season of Dancing With The Stars. He is known for his performances in musical theater and television.
  • Charli D'Amelio: Charli D'Amelio is a social media influencer and dancer who gained fame on TikTok. She competed in the 31st season of Dancing With The Stars and is known for her dance skills and large following.
  • Hannah Brown: Hannah Brown is a television personality and former Miss Alabama USA. She competed in the 28th season of Dancing With The Stars and is known for her bubbly personality and dance performances.
